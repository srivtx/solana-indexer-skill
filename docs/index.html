The problem

Every serious Solana dApp — DeFi dashboards, NFT marketplaces, gaming leaderboards, social graphs, analytics — needs a custom indexer. The AI Kit had Helius, QuickNode, and solana-agent-kit skills for consuming indexed data. Nothing for building one. Every founder had to rediscover the same patterns.
